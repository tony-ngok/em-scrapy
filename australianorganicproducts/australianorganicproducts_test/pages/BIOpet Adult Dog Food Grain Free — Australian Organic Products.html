BIOpet's dog food aims to focus on the dog’s natural diet and therefore produced a grain free pet food for less skin and digestive problems due to no grain or glutens. BIOpet adult dog food provides protein for lean muscle growth and includes natural prebiotics which help to develop a healthy digestive system. The formula is rich in Omega 3 & Omega 6 fatty acids to promote healthy skin and a shiny coat.

Buy BIOpet Adult Dog Food Grain Free online and save!

Biopet Adult Grain Free Dog Food offers a complete & balanced Grain Free formula. It sources proteins from Australian chicken & beef growers, with the inclusion of digestible carbohydrates such as potato & tapioca.

Features

  • GM Free
  • Grain Free
  • Gluten Free
  • Free from wheat, sorghum, corn & soy.

Ingredients

Beef meal, chicken meal, field peas, potatoes, poultry oil, tapioca, canola, fishmeal, flaxseed, beet pulp, fish oil, salt, natural prebiotic, yucca schidigera extract, taurine, mixed tocopherols (vitamin E), rosemary plant extract, vitamins (vitamins A, D, E, K, B1, B2, B6, B12, niacin, folic acid, biotin, choline, pantothenic acid), minerals (iron, zinc, copper, manganese, selenium, iodine, cobalt), lutein, garlic and kelp
