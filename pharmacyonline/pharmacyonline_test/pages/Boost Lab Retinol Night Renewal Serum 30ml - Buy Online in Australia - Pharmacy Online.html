Boost Lab Retinol Night Renewal Serum 30ml

Boost Lab Retinol Night Renewal Serum 30ml

Forsigns of ageing

+ De-wrinkles & firms

+ Hydrates & illuminates

Our Night Renewal Serum is a night-time skin hydration formula designed to reduce skin damage that causes wrinkles, dry skin, loss of firmness and elasticity, and uneven pigmentation. Its the perfect renewal potion that works its magic, plumping and hydrating while you sleep so you can wake up with revitalised skin.

Scientifically developed using the latest Gold Standard retinol and granactive retinoids plus:

  • With soothing organic Aloe Vera and Bisabolol
  • Dermatologically tested
  • Oil free

Just the essential ingredients that each serve a purpose all at an affordable price point.

Used in combination with all your existing favourite moisturisers and cleansers, BOOST LAB serums gives your skin the boost it needs, when it needs it most.

You can buy Boost Lab Retinol Night Renewal Serum 30ml online in Australia from Pharmacy Online

Directions For Use

Just cleanse, apply and let it soak in!

  1. Cleanse your skin as usual morning and/or evening
  2. Apply one drop to your skin and rub in
  3. Let the serum soak in and work its magic

The Ordinary's formula has tasmannial lanceolata extract instead of aloe vera. In terms of texture, I like them both. However, Boost Lab's offering is 30ml for $29.95. whereas The Ordinary is 30ml for $17.90. I feel that Boost Lab could've done more to their formula to not be almost the same as The Ordinary's. At the moment I see no reason to choose Boost Lab's version over The Ordinary's.
